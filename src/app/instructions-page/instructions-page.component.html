THE INSTRUCTIONS

"The Button" is a social experiment brought to light by popular YouTuber Mumbo Jumbo, first seen on Episode 14 - The Button of his popular Hermitcraft 7 series. Mumbo Jumbo and his fellow hermits make great Minecraft content that I highly reccomend checking out.

Mumbo explains "the button" like so:

( slightly edited to fit this implementation)

What is The Button? The button is alive and has to be kept alive by being pressed. The closer to death the button is when you save it, the higher 'rank' you get. When a user has an account, your rank is signified by the colored banner to the left of your display name.

How does The Button work? If you press the button, the timer resets, and you will gain the colored rank that the timer was at when you pressed the button.

Do I get a prize for saving it in red? Nope! But everyone will think you are awesome. And purple team members will bow down to you. Should you laugh at people who are a lower rank than you? YES! 100% YES.

It's all fun and games, but people on purple team should be ridiculed.

Long live the button!

Extra Information

Authorization is handled through Auth0, a leader in security providers. No personal information is stored on the app's database.

The source code for the client application can be found at github.com/jsainz237/the-button, and the server source code can be found at github.com/jsainz237/the-button-server.

Bugs and/or suggestions can be emailed to the.button.suggestions@gmail.com. I will try to keep up to date and add new features every so often. However I have other projects in the works at the moment, so I will not be updating on a regular basis.
