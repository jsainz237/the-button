THE INSTRUCTIONS

"The Button" is a social experiment first created by popular YouTuber Mumbo Jumbo, first seen on Episode 14 - The Button of his popular Hermitcraft 7 series. Mumbo Jumbo and his fellow hermits make great Minecraft content that I highly reccomend checking out.

Mumbo explains "the button" like so:

( slightly edited to fit this implementation)

What is The Button? The button is alive and has to be kept alive by being pressed. The closer to death the button is when you save it, the higher 'rank' you get. When a user has an account, your rank is signified by the colored banner to the left of your display name.

How does The Button work? If you press the button, the timer resets, and you will gain the colored rank that the timer was at when you pressed the button.

Do I get a prize for saving it in red? Nope! But everyone will think you are awesome. And purple team members will bow down to you. Should you laugh at people who are a lower rank than you? YES! 100% YES.

It's all fun and games, but people on purple team should be ridiculed.

Long live the button!
